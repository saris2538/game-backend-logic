# Game Rules Overview

This game simulates decision-making under uncertainty.

## Phase 1: Bidding
- Teams bid on blocks of land with a fixed budget
- Highest bid wins the block
- Ties are allowed and resolved through shared ownership

## Phase 2: Production Selection
- Teams choose one production method per block
- More advanced methods cost more but have higher potential upside
- Actual outcomes are revealed at the end

## Scoring
- Profit or loss is calculated per block
- Tied winners share profit or loss equally
- Final rankings are based on total net profit
